for
Channel of Blessings Reiki Healing

By Paul Schultz, May 2020

Objective

Channel of Blessings is a newly opened Reiki Healing practice based out of Orange County, California. As they are a new business they need a set of logos to accurately convey their brand message for use on marketing materials. In addition to logos they are in need of a website that will be easy for their users to interact with and book appointments as needed.

Color Palette

As the business is a Reiki practice there is a strong emphasis on spirituality for the branding, CoB has also expressed a desire for the website to be very colorful. So I have decided to employ the use of all of the colors of the seven chakras to be used in the logos and the site.

Logos

Making use of the broad color palette I have created five different logos containing symbols representing spirituality and growth.
